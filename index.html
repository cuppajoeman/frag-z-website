frag-z

the art of hitting clips

y'know when you're in that lobby - strafing around at mach 10 trying to hit the sickest shots possible while you make yourself and others dizzy? it's the best. games that allow you to move around like this are a dying craft. the best breakneck shooters are on their way out. servers are few and far between, filled with cheaters or a couple washed pros who are too fucking good. we're trying to bring this kind of gameplay back with frag-z.

progress

principles

at its core, frag-z is a multiplayer shooter with a focus on movement and fluidity. graphics will be minimal (sorta pixelated/bitcrushed) with performance in mind. we're thinking of a super high speed cap so that players can go mad with classic techniques like strafing, c-jumps, and edge bugs. there will be a bunch of weapons for players to use, each one with a distinct playstyle. the gist is to reward the kind of gameplay you'd see in a 2009 nitecore frag movie.

weapons

  • melee
    • close up, slow rate, no knockback
  • shot gun
    • close/mid range, medium rate, high knockback
  • lightning gun
    • mid range, medium rate, low knockback
  • rocket launcher
    • close/mid range, slow rate, higher knockback
  • rail gun
    • medium/long range, slow rate, medium knockback

most points win (mpw)

for now we're working on a Most Points Win (MPW) sort of thing. in this mode, players have infinite ammo and can use any weapon. kills will award points on the basis of the weapon used and how nice the shot was (we're trying for a clear system here - join the discord to suggest what sorts of shots should be rewarded most). the matches will last for 10 minutes and the player/team with the most points wins.

scoring

  • melee
    • consecutive hits
    • back stab
  • shot gun
    • meat shot
  • lightning gun
    • consecutive shots hit
    • pinning
  • rocket launcher
    • air shots
  • rail gun
    • high points for close up and long range
